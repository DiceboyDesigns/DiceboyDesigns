About Me

Hi! You can call me Crow,
I'm a graphic design and studio art student at Keene State College.

I work with a variety of media including, but not limited to:

  • Charcoal drawing
  • Acrylic painting
  • Watercolor painting
  • Digital painting
  • Digital sticker design
