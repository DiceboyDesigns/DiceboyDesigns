Commision Info and Pricing Guides

I accept payment via paypal or venmo, and require half payment to be delivered upfront and the other half to be payed upon delivery of the artwork.

Sticker Commissions

Sticker desings cost $20 per design, plus the purchase of the sticker itself. After recieving your request, I will send you several mock-ups, adjust as requested, and verify the design with you before publishing to redbubble. I accept sticker commissions for logos, fanworks, memes, as well as other custom designs.
